Ornithopter Design and Creation

What is an Ornithopter?

Ornithopters are machines designed to replicate the flight of birds. It utilizes a crank to move a shaft to power its wings.

How I made my ornithopter

I first produced the framing for my ornithopter using lightweight balsa wood. I then made the crank mechanism by bending a paperclip into an u-shape to let the wings flap. I then attached the arms allowing the wings to flap with the rotating crank. After finisnhing the crank, I then moved onto creating the wings. I made the braces for the wings from plywood then made the actual wing out of a very light paper. They were designed to assist the ornithopter in gliding down to the floor. The tail for the ornithopter was made with the same type of paper.

Challenges

While creating the ornithopter I had very few challenges. Origional crank mechanism that I created had a large friction problem and was unable to rotate freely. I solved this by adding beads on the crank. to minimise contact with the hot glue.
