KAMEHAMEHA

My Automata

I am in the process of making an automata that performs a human action.

Goku

Goku is a saiyan, a super-human who has unusual powers.

Action

My Goku will perform a "Kamehameha", an action where he charges up a ball of power and then fires it at an enemy.

Problems: Day 1

I encountered many problems during the first day of building. I created a prototype of the body, but it did not have a long enough arm to reach the other side. The mechanism to move Goku's arms and legs was also difficult to think of.

Problems: Day 2

During my second day of building, I created the mechanism to move Goku's arms and legs. A rod was stuck through a side of a box and ended with a gear in the middle of the space. A copper wire was stuck through the rod, and a thin vertical rod was stuck through the wire. This thin rod went up through the top of the box, and will be attached to a string that is attached to the arms of Goku. Thinking of the idea of the wire and holes required to let the vertical rod move freely was a minor problem. Also, placing the different pieces in the correct ways and places was essential to having a working mechanism.
