How I Made an Omithopter

During class I created an omithopter made out of sticks, staws, paperclips, and paper wings. An omithopter is a machine designed to achieve flight by means of flapping wings. I created a machine that flapped its wings when the hand lever was pulled and flies when you throw it into the air. We studied how birds fly and the pivot point of objects in order to help us build our models.

Challenge I came across:

One of the problems I faced when making the omithopter was time. I had limited time to finish the project that I was unable to make my omithopter completely work. This caused my omithopter to not fly as it was suppose to, instead it would flap its wings and fall directly towards the ground.
