How I made An Ornithopter

>

Intro

What is an ornithopther?

An ornithopther is a mechical device that has wings powered by rubber band.
>

My project

To build a workable ornithopther.

My steps

First: In my mind and my notebook, I came up with idea of how it fly,how it look and how it was build.
Second: Grab the material and start building

My Challenge

My challenge was to make a mechanism that flaping the wings and finding and making a stable, light wing. I have to use pliers to bend the metal as straght as possible. As for my wings, I find very light wings and cut it in the way half-circle and strenghten it by straw.
